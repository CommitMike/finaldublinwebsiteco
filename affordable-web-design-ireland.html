Affordable does not mean vague

The way to keep a website affordable is to agree the scope and avoid unnecessary extras at the beginning.

  • Clear page count and project goals
  • Simple design system instead of overbuilding
  • Core SEO basics included
  • Support period explained up front

When to start simple

Many businesses do not need a complex first website. They need a professional presence that can grow later.

  • New businesses testing their offer
  • Sole traders who need enquiries
  • Outdated sites that need a clean rebuild
  • Businesses that want to launch quickly

How to keep website costs sensible

Costs rise when a project has unclear goals, too many pages, custom features that are not needed yet, or repeated changes after the direction has already been agreed.

We keep the project focused on what customers need first: understanding your business, trusting it enough to enquire, and finding the right way to contact you.
