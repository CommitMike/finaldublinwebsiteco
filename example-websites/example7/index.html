Our Story

A little corner of
warmth in Rathmines.

The Copper Kettle started as a dream between two sisters, Aoife and Sinéad, who wanted to bring real café culture to their neighbourhood. No chains, no shortcuts — just proper coffee, made with care.

Fifteen years on, we're still sourcing our beans from the same Dublin roaster, still baking our scones fresh every morning, and still trying to remember everyone's order before they reach the counter.

Single-Origin Coffee Roasted locally, brewed with precision
Baked Fresh Daily Everything out of our kitchen by 8am
Always Welcoming Laptops, dogs & prams all welcome
Local & Seasonal Suppliers within 60km where possible
