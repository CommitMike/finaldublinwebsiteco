Our Approach

Therapy that starts
with listening.

We believe that every person holds the capacity for growth and healing. Our therapists don't arrive with a script — they arrive with genuine curiosity, warmth, and the training to help you find your own way forward.

We draw on a range of evidence-based approaches — including CBT, psychodynamic therapy, and person-centred counselling — tailored to you, not a one-size-fits-all model.

FAQ

Questions you
might have.

If something is causing you distress — whether it's a specific event, an ongoing pattern, or just a vague sense that things could be better — therapy can help. You don't need to be in crisis to benefit. Many clients come to us simply wanting to understand themselves better or make changes they've been putting off.

There's no fixed answer. Some people find 6–10 sessions address what they came for. Others prefer longer-term work. We review regularly and you're always in control of when to pause or finish. There's no pressure to continue beyond what feels right for you.

Yes. Everything shared in sessions is confidential. The only exceptions are situations where there is a serious risk of harm to yourself or others — these are explained clearly in our client agreement before we begin. We follow the IACP Code of Ethics in full.

Yes. All of our therapists offer secure video sessions via a GDPR-compliant platform. Online therapy is equally effective for most concerns and many clients prefer it for the flexibility. We can also mix in-person and online sessions if that suits your schedule.

Some health insurance plans cover psychotherapy. We are registered with Laya Healthcare and Irish Life Health. We recommend checking your policy directly — we can provide receipts for all sessions to support a claim. We do not currently work directly with VHI.

It's a relaxed 20-minute phone or video call — not a session, just a conversation. You can tell us a little about what's brought you here, ask any questions, and get a feel for whether it seems like a good fit. There's no pressure at all to book anything afterwards.
