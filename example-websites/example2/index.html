Our Story

Three generations.
One standard.

Malone's Garage opened on Crumlin Road in 1987. Seamus Malone started it with a single ramp and a reputation built on word of mouth. His son Declan joined in 2003, and Declan's daughter Orla completed her apprenticeship with us in 2021.

We've never chased growth for the sake of it. We're still the same workshop — just with better tools, more experience, and a lot of long-term customers who've been bringing us their cars for decades.
