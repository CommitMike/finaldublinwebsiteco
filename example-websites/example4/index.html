Our Story

Good ingredients,
honestly made.

The Dublin Larder started at a farmers' market stall in Dún Laoghaire in 2019. We were frustrated by supermarket jams full of sugar and sauces full of stuff you couldn't pronounce.

So we started making our own — using Irish ingredients wherever possible, in proper small batches, with nothing added that didn't need to be there. People kept asking where they could buy them, so here we are.
