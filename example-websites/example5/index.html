About the Clinic

Physio the way
it should be.

Apex Physio was founded in 2014 with one philosophy: treat the cause, not just the symptom. No rushed appointments, no generic exercises — just thorough assessment and evidence-based treatment tailored to you.

Our clinic on Ranelagh Road sees everyone from weekend runners to post-operative patients to desk workers with chronic neck pain. You don't need a GP referral to book.
