Who We Are

NO
SHORTCUTS.
EVER.

Slice & Co. was born out of a simple frustration — bad pizza in a city that deserved better. We started with one wood-fired oven, a recipe from Marco's grandmother in Napoli, and an obsession with getting the dough right.

Every base is proved for 48 hours. Every sauce is made the same day. Every pizza goes into a 450°C oven. You can taste the difference.
