02Our Story

A café that grows
with the seasons.

Stems & Grounds began as a flower stall and a coffee hatch on Blackrock Market. Five years later, we've grown into a full café — but the ethos hasn't changed. We source what's fresh, we brew what's good, and we let the seasons do the talking.

Our kitchen changes its specials weekly based on what's available from local producers. The flowers on your table were arranged that morning. The coffee on your cup was roasted a few days ago, not months.
