About Volt & Sons

A family business
built on doing it right.

Volt & Sons was founded in 2010 by Paddy Doyle, a master electrician with over 20 years in the trade. What started as a one-van operation in Dublin 7 is now a team of eight, covering all of Dublin and parts of Wicklow and Kildare.

We're father-and-sons — Paddy, Ciarán and Oisín — and we treat every job like it's in our own home. That means proper cable management, clean finishes, and being honest when something needs doing differently to what was quoted.
