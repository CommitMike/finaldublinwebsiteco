Common signs

  • Your site is awkward on phones
  • Contact details are hard to find
  • The design no longer matches your business
  • Pages feel slow, cluttered or unclear

What we improve

A redesign is not only a visual refresh. It is a chance to fix the structure and make the site easier to use.

  • Cleaner layout and stronger calls-to-action
  • Updated service copy and page flow
  • Mobile-first responsive design
  • Basic technical and on-page SEO cleanup

How we approach it

We review what you already have, keep what still works and rebuild what is holding the site back.

  • Review current pages and content
  • Plan the new structure before design
  • Build and revise with clear checkpoints
  • Launch with redirects or updated links where needed

Keep the useful parts, remove the friction

A good redesign keeps your business recognisable while making the website easier to read, navigate and act on. For local businesses, that often means clearer service pages, stronger contact routes and a simpler homepage.

If you already have rankings or indexed pages, redesign work should be handled carefully so useful URLs and content are not thrown away without reason.
